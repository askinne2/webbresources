Clubs

Here at Webb, we have many clubs that suit all interests. Clubs are grouped into A, B, and C sections. Every Wednesday, either A, B, or C clubs meet, switching each week. Meetings are 30 minutes. During these times, sponsors and club leaders come up with interactive activities for members to do. Some clubs are fun and carefree, like orchestra, outdoors club, and mahjong. Others are competitive, like Technology Student Association, Science Olympiad, and Health Occupations Students of America. Members in these extracurriculars compete in various events to win medals.

Curriculum Guide

Our Webb curriculum guide outlines all the classes available for students and qualifications to graduate. For example, four years of engligh and math are required, as well as three years of science and social studies. one year of fine arts and one credit (three seasons) of physical education is also required. At Webb, we care a lot about service and helping our community, so students need 60 service hours (fifteen each year) to graduate. We offer many classes at different levels to ensure that all students can succeed.

Handbook

Webb has many core values and expectations we try to uphold every day. Our handbook outlines those values as well as overall rules and expectations. Striving for excellence, honor, integrity, care, and community are some of the defining qualities of our school. Forms of communication, directories, volunteering, health and safety, transportation, dining, student support, academics, athletics, rules, uniforms, college counseling, and attendance are some major topics covered in the handbook.
