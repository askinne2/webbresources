What You Can Expect

Welcome to your Webb School of Knoxville student portal! On this site, you will find many resources to help make your school day easier. We encourage you to take a glance at our current schedule for the 2025 - 2026 school year. We have also provided the new schedule for the 2026 - 2027 school year, as well as some details on why we have decided to change it. There is also a page for clubs and sports that includes when and where they meet. We have added the academic and athletic calenders so students can start planning their year. The curriculum guide will help you choose classes, and the handbook consists of general rules and standard procedures. Don't forget to take a look at our resources highlight page, and send us some feedback of what we can add in the future!
